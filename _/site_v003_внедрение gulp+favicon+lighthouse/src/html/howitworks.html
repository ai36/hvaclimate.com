How it works?

Get a free consultation

Contact us by phone, email, or submit a request on the website. Please specify the model of the equipment, a description of the problem, and your preferred date and time for the specialist's visit. Our customer service representative can provide you with information about the estimated cost of services and schedule a date for a specialist's visit.

Preparing for the specialist's visit

Ensure that access to the equipment is clear and safe for conducting diagnostics and repairs. Prepare all necessary documentation for your equipment, if available.

Diagnostic and estimation

The specialist will diagnose the equipment, determine the cause of the malfunction, and prepare an estimate for the repair. Discuss the details of the repair, timelines, and cost with the technician.

Quality check of completed work

After the repair is completed, the specialist should test the equipment's functionality in various modes. Ensure there are no gas leaks or other malfunctions.

Payment for Services

Make the payment in accordance with the approved estimate. Payment can be made in cash, by credit card, through online payments, or by check. You can read more about payment options here.

Feedback

After the work is completed, please leave a review about the quality of service and the repair performed. This will help us improve our service.
