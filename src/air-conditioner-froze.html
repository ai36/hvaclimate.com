A frozen air conditioner can be a major inconvenience, causing your home to become uncomfortable and potentially leading to more serious issues with your HVAC system. There are several reasons why your air conditioner might freeze, including low refrigerant levels, restricted airflow, or issues with the blower motor. Understanding these causes is the first step in resolving the problem and ensuring your air conditioner operates efficiently.
