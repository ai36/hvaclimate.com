Expert Electric Furnace Repair Services

When your electric furnace stops working, it can leave your home cold and uncomfortable. Fortunately, our experienced technicians in Portland and Vancouver are here to help. We provide prompt, reliable electric furnace repair services to keep your home warm and cozy all winter long.

Common Electric Furnace Problems

  • Not Heating: If your furnace is not producing heat, it could be due to a faulty heating element or thermostat issues.
  • Frequent Cycling: Frequent on and off cycling might indicate a clogged filter or a malfunctioning thermostat.
  • Blower Issues: A continuously running blower could signal problems with the limit switch.

Our Electric Furnace Repair Process

We start with a comprehensive diagnosis to identify the exact cause of your furnace's issue. Once diagnosed, our technicians will:

  • Explain the problem and discuss the best solutions available.
  • Proceed with the repairs once we have your approval.
  • Test the furnace post-repair to ensure it is running efficiently.
