Why Do You Need To Replace Your Electric Furnace?

Replacing an aging or malfunctioning electric furnace can improve your home's energy efficiency, comfort, and safety. At HVA Climate Control, we offer expert electric furnace replacement services in Portland and Vancouver, ensuring that your new furnace is installed with precision and care.

Signs It’s Time to Replace Your Electric Furnace

  • Old Age: Furnaces older than 15 years are typically less efficient and may require frequent repairs.
  • High Energy Bills: Rising energy costs may indicate your furnace is working harder than it should.
  • Frequent Breakdowns: If you're experiencing frequent breakdowns, it may be more cost-effective to replace rather than repair.

Our Electric Furnace Replacement Process

  • Evaluate your current system and recommend the best replacement options.
