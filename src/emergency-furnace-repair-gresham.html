Common Furnace Problems Requiring Immediate Attention

Furnace issues can arise at any time, especially during the colder months. Some common problems that need urgent repair include:

  • No Heat Production: Furnace is running but not producing heat.
  • Strange Noises: Unusual sounds like banging or squealing indicating mechanical problems.
  • Gas Odors: Smell of gas could indicate a leak and requires immediate attention.
  • Frequent Cycling: Furnace turns on and off frequently, affecting efficiency.
