Common Emergency Furnace Issues We Handle

  • No Heat Production: Furnace not generating heat despite being turned on.
  • Strange Noises: Unusual sounds like banging or squealing indicating mechanical issues.
  • Gas Odors: The smell of gas could indicate a leak and requires immediate attention.
  • Frequent Cycling: Furnace turning on and off rapidly, affecting efficiency.
