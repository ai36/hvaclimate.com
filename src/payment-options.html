Financing Options for Heating and Cooling Systems

Don't Let Price Stand in the Way of Your Comfort

We all want our homes to be cozy and comfortable, but sometimes the cost of a new heating or cooling system can seem overwhelming. The good news is that HVA Climate Control offers flexible financing options, so you can enjoy the perfect indoor climate without straining your budget.

How Much Does a New System Cost?

The cost of a new system depends on many factors: the type of equipment, the complexity of the installation, and your individual needs. Don't worryâ€”we always provide a transparent estimate so you know exactly what to expect.

What Affects Installation Costs?

The installation price depends on:

  • The type of equipment you choose
  • Whether we're replacing an old system or installing a new one from scratch
  • The installation location (attic, basement, garage, etc.)
  • The condition of existing ductwork and electrical systems

Approximate Prices

To help you plan, here are some estimated price ranges:

  • Gas Furnaces: from $5,500 to $8,000
  • Electric Furnaces: from $5,000 to $8,000
  • Central Air Conditioners: from $7,000 to $10,000
  • Hybrid Heating Systems: from $13,000 to $20,000

Finding the Perfect System for You

Choosing the right system isn't just about the price. It's important that it fits your home and lifestyle. We consider all the nuances:

  • The size and layout of your home
  • Window placement and sunlight exposure
  • Ceiling height
  • Insulation levels
  • And much more

Our specialists will help you select a system of optimal size and capacity to avoid overpaying for energy and to prevent frequent breakdowns.

How Can We Help with Financing?

We understand that purchasing a new system is a significant decision. That's why we offer:

  • Flexible Payment Plans: Choose a schedule that suits you best.
  • Competitive Rates: We partner with trusted financial institutions to offer the best terms.
  • Discounts and Promotions: We always keep our clients informed about current special offers to help you save.

Financing for Air Conditioners and Heat Pumps

Dreaming of cool comfort on hot summer days? We offer financing options for new air conditioners and heat pumps. By choosing energy-efficient models, you not only save on your electricity bills but may also qualify for additional rebates from utility companies.

Financing for Heating Systems

Winter is around the corner, and your old furnace isn't keeping up? We'll help you upgrade your heating system without financial stress. Whether it's a gas or electric furnace, we'll find the optimal solution and offer convenient payment options.

Why Invest in a New System Now?

  • Comfort and Coziness: With a new system, your home will be warm in winter and cool in summer.
  • Energy Savings: Modern equipment is more energy-efficient, reducing your monthly expenses.
  • Increase Home Value: Energy-efficient systems make your home more attractive to potential buyers.
